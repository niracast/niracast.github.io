FAQs

faq

+ What is WaveFi ?
WaveFi is a portable hotspot wireless WiFi device that allows you to stay connected on the go, without the need for a SIM card. It uses Cloud SIM technology to create a WLAN wherever you are, connecting up to 10 devices simultaneously.
+ How does WaveFi work without a SIM card ?
WaveFi uses Cloud SIM technology, which allows it to automatically connect to available local networks. This eliminates the need for a physical SIM card and ensures reliable connectivity wherever you go.
+ What is the range of WaveFi ?
WaveFi provides a strong WiFi signal within a range of up to 50 meters, depending on the environment and network conditions.
+ Is there any special offer for prebooking ?
Yes! When you prebook WaveFi, you can enjoy exclusive benefits, such as free 40 Mbps unlimited internet for a year at a special price of ₹1,000.
+ Can I use WaveFi internationally ?
Yes! WaveFi uses Cloud SIM technology to connect to local networks worldwide, making it perfect for international travel.
+ Is there a warranty on WaveFi ?
Yes, WaveFi comes with a one-year warranty. If you encounter any issues with your device, our customer support team will assist you.We are here to assist you with troubleshooting and, if needed, offer a replacement or repair under warranty.
