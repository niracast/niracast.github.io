FAQs

For Better Understanding

1. What is Nira ?
Nira is a smart digital advertising network that places NiraCast screens on store counters to display video ads — and pays store owners ₹ 1,500/month.
2. What do store owners have to do ?
Just keep the screen on your counter and plugged in. Ads run automatically — no effort needed from your side. Ad categorization, updation, scheduling, geo-targeting, screen time tracking and status control — everything is automated.
3. Is there any cost to get the screen ?
Only ₹ 1,000/- refundable security deposit is required. You get it back when you return the screen in working condition.
4. When will I receive the screen after registration ?
Your screen will be delivered and installed within 5–7 working days after registration and payment.
5. How do I get the ₹ 1,500 monthly payment ?
You will receive ₹ 1,500 every month directly into the bank account you provided during registration.
6. What if the screen gets damaged ?
Contact our support team. We’ll handle repair or replacement based on the issue. The contact number of your area's field agent will be provided in the welcome letter.
7. Can I return the screen later ?
Yes, you can return the screen anytime. If it’s in good working condition, your ₹1,000 security deposit will be fully refunded. Click here to return the screen.
8. What kind of ads will run on the screen ?
Only brand-safe, governmental, family-friendly video ads from trusted local and national advertisers.
