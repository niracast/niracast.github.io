FAQs

For Better Understanding

1. What is Nira ?
Nira is a smart digital advertising network that places NiraCast screens on store counters to display video ads — and pays you ₹1,500/month.
2. What do store owners have to do ?
Just keep the screen on your counter and plugged in. Ads run automatically — no effort needed from your side.
3. Is there any cost to get the screen ?
Only a ₹1,000 refundable security deposit is required. You get it back when you return the screen in working condition.
4. When will I receive the screen after registration ?
Your screen will be delivered and installed within 5–7 working days after registration and payment.
5. How do I get the ₹1,500 monthly payment ?
You’ll receive ₹1,500 every month directly via bank transfer or UPI.
6. What if the screen gets damaged ?
Contact our support team. We’ll handle repair or replacement based on the issue.
7. Can I return the screen later ?
Yes, you can return the screen anytime. If it’s in good working condition, your ₹1,000 deposit will be fully refunded.
8. What kind of ads will run on the screen ?
Only brand-safe, governmental, family-friendly video ads from trusted local and national advertisers.
